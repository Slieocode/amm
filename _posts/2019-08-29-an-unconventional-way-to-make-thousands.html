Want an unusual but extremely effective way of making thousands if not tens of thousands of dollars? Of course you do. I was at work a few months back now and a kid mentioned to me he was making 'thousands' in a day. I immediately assumed the worst, however, was quickly enlightened that he was making the money from this magical thing called support role castings.

Before you read on please check out our new YouTube channel here.

I had to check it out and immediately jumped online to apply for some myself. I found various sites (casting agencies) and started applying. I was able to be short-listed for three castings worth, two, twelve, and one thousand respectively within 2 months with almost no effort.

So you might be thinking, but I can't act. Yeah, well either can I but that is why I said support roles. These are where you often do not speak at all and are more or less a filler and get paid to do it. If, however, you can speak to a camera, you can apply for TV ads where you may have a star part and make the big bucks.
