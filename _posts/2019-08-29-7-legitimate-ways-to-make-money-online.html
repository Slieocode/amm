E-commerce

This can be Amazon FBA, drop shipping or any other method. While this one can be a bit hard to learn, there is a wealth of knowledge online today. Particularly on YouTube due to the rise of Shopify drop shipping YouTubers. Though please be careful with those trying to sell their courses, most are NOT worth your money.

I personally dropship using Shopify and while I am yet to make thousands a day, I have been able to turn over up to $160 a day in very early days.

Influencer accounts

Influencer accounts refers to using social media accounts with a following to run promotions for cash. I personally created two of these on Instagram and was able to get some easy cash from it. I was hacked when I was starting to get big and make some real money, so I never got to see much more than $5-10 per promotion. However, as someone who often pays influencers to advertise my products, I can confidently say they make some great money for simply posting a product or service endorsement. I sometimes pay upwards of $100 per post.

eBooks/Courses

If you have a skill of some sort you can probably teach it online via a course, eBook or similar means. I have done this myself with an eBook in a very niche area. Examples that are common right now are Shopify courses, blogging and digital marketing. A LOT of people are making courses on these topics, and unfortunately a lot of them are bogus snake oil. Please do not become one of those guys. I like to suggest finding a niche area that you are interested and skilled in and making quality work that people will thank you for. My eBook has nothing to do with anything online like most are. You can make a course on literally anything as long as there is a demand for it.

Similar concept to above. By investing in stocks (wisely) whether it be long or short term you can make some extra income online.
